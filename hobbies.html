My Hobbies

This is actually an interesting topic, because I'm moving more and more to a merger between official things I for work, for example, and things I do as pastimes, like blockchain and cryptocurrency. I will therefore only list things I really do aside to relax.

  1. Hiking, walking out in the green. Preferably in deep virgin pine forests, where the scent is cool and fragrant.
  2. Swimming - I have generally always liked being in water, although ironically I nearly got swept away by the current at a young age, so I do stay wary of the depths. I keep saying I would like to go and freedive with dolphins and freedive more often in general, but I'm not quite certain I still want this. To be tried out and assessed...
  3. Flying my kite. I have a dear old Zammo Stunter kite which comes from South Africa and is 30 years old, and gratefully, bar a few pieces of duct tape and a new spar, flies very well. I would love to get or make a proper basic single line kite, as well as a dual line parasail-type kite to learn traction.
  4. Windsurfing - I haven't done this in ages, but feel this would be great fun now.
  5. Motorbiking - I used to have a motorbike, but just didn't use it anymore, and now if I could get one, I think it would be a Harley Davidson, to be able to ride low and slow whilst feeling like I'm speeding, and getting real good torque out of it.
